Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

1.The short version

MegRewards collects almost nothing, because it needs almost nothing. No payment details, no deposits, no ID documents, no KYC — this is a rewards and leaderboard site, not a casino. What we do hold is the minimum needed to run leaderboards, points and giveaways for the community, and we never sell it.

2.What we collect

  • Your Kick username — already public on Kick — when you chat, join giveaways, or earn community points.
  • The casino username you choose to submit when verifying for wager-based perks or joining a leaderboard under Meg's code. We use it only to read wager totals from that casino's affiliate statistics.
  • Activity inside the community — points earned and spent, giveaway entries, store redemptions, leaderboard standings.
  • Optionally, your Discord account if you link it for verification or giveaway roles.

Leaderboard displays use the names provided by the casino's own API — where a casino masks player names (e.g. Be***x), we display them masked.

3.What we never collect

  • No credit cards, bank details, or crypto wallet private keys.
  • No government ID, passports, or address documents — KYC belongs to the casinos you play on, under their own privacy policies.
  • No tracking ads, no ad networks, no selling or renting data to anyone, ever.

4.Where it lives & who touches it

Site data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure (Google Firebase) operated for MegRewards by WenBot, the platform this site runs on. Casino affiliate APIs are queried only to verify wagers/standings for the casino username you submitted. None of these providers are permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

This site uses no third-party advertising or analytics cookies. Your browser's local storage is used only for functional preferences (like a login session or UI settings).

5.Your choices

  • Removal: want your casino link, points, or history deleted? Message Meg on the Kick channel or community Discord and it will be removed.
  • Leaderboards: if you'd rather not appear, don't verify under the code — or ask to be removed and you will be.
  • Third-party sites we link to (Kick, Discord, casinos) have their own privacy policies that apply once you're there.

6.Changes & contact

If this policy changes meaningfully, the update will be announced on stream or Discord and the date above will change. Questions? Ask on the Kick channel — Meg answers.
