Overview — live stats: viewers chatting, points distributed, bot status, recent activity.

👥 Team — Moderators ELITE

Add up to 2 moderators from Settings → 👥 Team (Elite feature). They keep their own WenBot login but get full dashboard access on your behalf. Every action is attributed to them in the Activity Log.
Mods can: manage giveaways, store, raffles, verified users, points, portal customization, Discord settings, view audit log.
Mods can't: change billing, reconnect Kick, add/remove other mods, export your data.
The mod must already have a WenBot account (free Starter is fine). After you add them they need to log out and back in once for the access to take effect.

🛠 Troubleshooting

"Bot is offline" banner won't go away
Click Reconnect Kick in Settings. If WenBot was unmodded in your Kick chat, /mod WenBot to fix.
Viewer says "!verify didn't work"
Have them open wenbot.gg/verify.html?channel=<you> directly and sign in with Kick. Bot-issued tokens expire after 10 minutes.
Points aren't being earned
Check the Points page — min message length too high? Activity window too short? Viewers must send a qualifying message inside the window.
Public store / portal shows old items
Public pages cache for ~60s. Refresh after a minute.
Mod can't log in after being added
They need to log out and back in once — Firebase only refreshes their token after a new sign-in.
Still stuck? Email support@logicplaystudios.com with your Kick channel name + a screenshot of what you're seeing.
