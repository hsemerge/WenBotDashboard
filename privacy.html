Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WenBot ("the Service," "we," "us," or "our"), operated by LogicPlay Studios, collects, uses, stores, shares, and retains information when you use WenBot — including streamer dashboards, public streamer portals (such as wenbot.gg/<channel> and white-label custom domains), and any feature that involves signing in with Kick or Discord.

1. Who This Applies To

WenBot serves two kinds of users:

  • Streamers — content creators who create a WenBot account to run bots, giveaways, points, leaderboards, and store features for their community.
  • Viewers — members of a streamer's audience who interact with a streamer's WenBot portal, for example by signing in with Kick to verify their account, view their points balance, spend points in a streamer's store, enter a giveaway, or take part in a bonus battle or tournament.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

We take the security of your information seriously and apply industry-standard safeguards:

  • Server-side data (account info, points, redemptions, links) is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore), protected by access rules and served over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.
  • Kick access tokens are handled with care. When you sign in, your token is held in your own browser's local storage for your active session and is sent only to our servers to verify your identity. Where a sign-in must pass between our main site and a white-label custom domain, we use a single-use code that expires within 60 seconds — your token is never exposed in a shareable link.
  • Sensitive credentials — such as our own API keys and OAuth client secrets — are kept server-side only and are never exposed to your browser.
  • Authorized staff access. Authorized WenBot administrators, and any moderators you invite to your own account, may access your account to provide support, maintain and troubleshoot the Service, and investigate security or abuse. Administrative access is limited to a small allowlist, requires a verified login, is used only for these purposes, and is recorded in an internal audit log.
  • We never sell your personal data, and we do not transfer your profile or activity data to advertising networks, data brokers, or other monetization services.

No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure. If we become aware of a security incident affecting your information, we will take prompt action and provide any notifications required by law.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and operate the features you choose to use.
  • To attribute points, giveaway entries, store purchases, and competition entries to the correct viewer.
  • To display public leaderboards, store items, and winners on streamer portals.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the Service and prevent fraud or abuse.

5. How We Share Information

We share information only as needed to run the Service:

  • With the streamer whose community you interact with — for example, your Kick username, points balance, and redemptions appear in that streamer's dashboard so they can fulfill rewards.
  • With service providers that operate parts of the Service on our behalf, such as Google Firebase (hosting/database), Stripe (streamer payments), and Netlify (hosting). These providers process data under their own terms and only as needed to provide their service.
  • With Kick and Discord to the extent you authorize a connection through their official login systems.
  • When required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of WenBot, our users, or the public.

We do not use data obtained through the Kick API to target you with marketing or commercial offers outside of Kick, and we do not sell or license your data to third parties.

6. Data Retention

  • Kick access tokens are short-lived and expire automatically; the temporary single-use sign-in codes described above expire within 60 seconds.
  • Account and activity data is retained while your (or the relevant streamer's) account is active and as needed to provide the Service.
  • You may request deletion of your data as described below. Streamers can also export their data from the dashboard.

7. Your Choices and Rights

  • Log out at any time to clear your session from your browser.
  • Disconnect Kick or Discord by revoking WenBot's access in your Kick or Discord account settings.
  • Request access or deletion of your personal data by emailing support@logicplaystudios.com. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable data-protection laws.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

WenBot uses your browser's local storage to keep you signed in during a session and to remember preferences. We use only the minimum needed to operate the Service and do not use third-party advertising or behavioral-tracking cookies. Any session data is kept only as long as needed for your active session.

9. Children

WenBot is intended for users 18 years of age or older and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

WenBot is an independent service and is not endorsed by or affiliated with Kick beyond our use of Kick's official developer program.

11. Changes to This Policy

12. Contact
